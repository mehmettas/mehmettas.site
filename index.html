I have used MVVM architecture and the latest architecture components which are suggested by the Android. With Su iste, you could easily order your water and do the track of your order by the feature that enables live tracking of the courier. I have also completed the courier app of the Su iste.

Family Tracking App - Firebase MVVM Never Ending Background Services - Side Project

Creating a family and get joined into this family with your unique member id will give you a private family tracking experience as real-time with live location data that you continuously fetch. You could invite your all other family members into this family, you can track them as well as they track you.

Cent - Kotlin MVVM - Side Project

While developing this project, I focused on using all the benefits of Kotlin and applying it by following the software architectural pattern: MVVM. I tried to decrease the dependencies between my classes by applying Kotlin-Koin and for asynchronous programming I used Kotlin-Coroutines. After all, dealing with all this data, I used one of the android architecture component "LiveData" to make my application lifecycle aware.
